In Sweden we build amazing startups! Though founders are heroes, there is no success without brilliant tech executors. Let us shed some light secret heroes who are on the verge of becoming tech superstars.

3 categories

Top 10 developers

These guys and gals can code! They take ideas and create beautiful code, while breaking stuff to improve it. They might be passionate about open source, which is why they could have a Github or Stack Overflow profile filled with it. Great software developers tend to think: sharing is caring.

Top 10 designers/UX

Magnificent creatives strive to create inspirational experiences through simplicity and thoughtful details. They love dialogue and they find inspiration in the feedback from the users they aim to delight. They create great interfaces whilst always keeping in mind that less is more!

Top 10 product managers

Product vision is everything! They think the 'Lean Startup methodology' is gods gift to startups. They know how to prioritize resources, because they have themselves been producing techies. When someone has an idea they often shout: Thats a hypothesis - lets validate it!

Process

We have a three step process: (expired)

1. Nominate who you think are the best 30 under 30 tech executors before Sunday, October 31st, 2013.

2. A team of experienced developers, designers and tech headhunters will do the first screening, based on input from you and the nominee.

3. A jury, consisting of some of the most experienced Swedish tech startups, will make the final list composition.

PRIZE

The 30 winners will be part of a by-invitation-only network. Five events (all paid for) are planned during late 2013 and early 2014, where some of the best CTOs in Sweden and the world will take part, sharing their experiences. The people who nominated the best 30 people, will also be invited to the first of these sessions.

The 30 winners will also receive Amazon Web Services credits worth $10.000 (each!)

Ultimately: the next generation of tech stars will get to know the best executors!
